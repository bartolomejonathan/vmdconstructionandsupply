About Us

At VMD Construction and Supply, we are dedicated to providing top-quality construction services and supply solutions. With years of experience in the industry, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner

Company Background

Victor M. Daet Construction & Supply is owned and managed by Engr. Victor M. Daet when it was established on March 21, 1991 and was issued a contractors PCAB license no. 15621 on October 2, 1992. VMD Construction and Supply has been a trusted name in the construction industry for over two decades. We specialize in delivering top-notch construction services that meet and exceed our clients' expectations. Our team of skilled professionals is dedicated to ensuring the highest quality of workmanship and customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

We aim to be a diversified enterprise, principally engaged in the construction, providing cost-effective processes, products and services of superior quality, extending meaningful employment and better quality of life to our employess, delivering reasonable returns to our clients, and serving the welfare of the community at large

We will conduct our business with utmost professionalism; commit ourselves to total quality with never-ending improvements in our services to satisfy our customer. We will be a company to which each and every employee will be proud to belong

Our Vision

We will be a prestigious organization with professional operations and a partner in nation building, guided by the highest standards of competnece, integrity and excellence.

We will build finest project in the Philippines with the tradition of excellence, integrity, hard work, strenght through a trust worthy relationship with business partners and clients.
