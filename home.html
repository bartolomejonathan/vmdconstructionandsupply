VMD Construction and Supply

The company has completed different registered projects in Bicol Region and established its presence in the Metro Manila last 2003 and opened their office branch in Quezon City. In 2009, our office transferred to 184-186 Maysan Road, Maysan, Valenzuela City

The company specializes in the construction of various government projects such as Civil Works for Domestic Airports, Roadways, Flood Control, Buildings, and Water Pipelines.

The company will continue to contribute to the progress and growth of the country as it moves forward to the future towards delivering its outsourcing brand of services to its customers.
